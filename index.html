Arcade Instructions

Power and Volume

Underneath the control panel is a cabinet door. Firmly grasp the horizontal bar and pull.

On the right is a power strip with the switch facing toward you. Flip the switch to the right to turn on the arcade.

Volume is controlled by the silver box in the middle. Rotate the large knob on the right of the box to adjust the volume. The sound is muted with the button on the left of the box. The volume box will have a light on when the sound is on.

Selecting a Game

Use the on screen menu to select a game. The menu contains on-screen prompts, but here are some gotchas.

Selecting from Lists

Use the joystick to move to different items in the list. Move the joystick to the left and right to page up and page down, respectively. Select items with the A button, which is the upper button closest to the joystick.

Search and Browse

Press the Player 1 button to get a menu with options to browse by recents and other categories. You can also use this menu to search, buy you might have better luck searching with web list.
